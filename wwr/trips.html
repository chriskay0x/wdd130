Our Featured Expeditions

The Wild Salmon Run

Experience three days on the majestic Salmon River. Perfect for intermediate rafters, offering a mix of exciting Class III rapids and breathtaking scenic floats. Includes all gear, meals, and overnight camping.

Green River Gorge Day Trip

A high-energy, full-day trip suitable for beginners and families. Navigate continuous Class II-III rapids through a stunning narrow gorge. Lunch is provided riverside. No prior experience is necessary!

Grizzly Peak Expedition

An intense, two-day challenge for experienced rafters only. Features demanding Class IV and V rapids. You must be physically fit and confident in water. This is an adventure for serious thrill-seekers.
